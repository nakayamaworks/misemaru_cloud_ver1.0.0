Store Onboarding Guide

Follow this checklist to prepare, deploy, and verify your Misemaru Cloud store.

Preparation: essentials

  • Ensure you have a Google account with the required permissions.
  • Copy the template spreadsheet and configure script properties.
  • Collect branding assets, theme colors, supported languages, and contact details.

Deploy & verify

  • Deploy the GAS web app and note the execution URL.
  • Configure Google Identity Services and whitelist accounts if needed.
  • Submit your GAS ID to the registry and request verification.

Need help?

Reach out to the admin community or support@misemaru.cloud.
